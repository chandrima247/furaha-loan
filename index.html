Eligibility

Check If You're Eligible

Answer a few quick questions to see if you can apply. This takes less than a minute and does not affect your credit.

  • You are a Ugandan national aged 21 to 70.
  • You are paying school fees for a student.
  • You have an active mobile number and valid ID.
  • You are able to repay over a short-term plan.

What You'll Need

Prepare Before You Apply

Having these ready will help you complete your application smoothly.

Mobile Number and Verification

Your mobile number will be used to start and verify your application.

National ID (NIN)

You will need your NIN and a valid ID. You'll also be asked to scan your ID and take a live selfie.

Well-Lit Environment

Make sure you are in a well-lit place so your ID scan and selfie can be captured clearly.

Student Registration Details

Have the student's details ready through one of the supported systems: PegPay, SchoolPay, or SurePay.

Mobile Wallet

An active mobile wallet is required for repayment.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers on amount, fees, collateral, and repayment terms.

Furaha shows a loan range from UGX 200,000 to UGX 3,500,000. The final approved amount depends on your application and verification.
The loan is presented with 5% monthly interest and a 3% processing fee. Your repayment and total cost are shown before you accept the offer.
The product details provided do not state any explicit physical collateral requirement. Instead, repayment enforcement is centered on wallet-based repayment and auto-debit behavior.
There is no penalty for early repayment. If the loan becomes overdue, a 2.5% late penalty applies to the overdue amount.
