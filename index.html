Eligibility

Who Can Apply

Keep the criteria clear and simple so borrowers can self-qualify quickly before entering the full application flow.

Core requirements

  • Indian residents with valid government-issued ID.
  • Age between 21 and 60 years.
  • Salaried or self-employed individuals.
  • Active mobile number and bank account.

Important note

  • Final eligibility depends on profile verification and credit assessment.
  • Offer amount, tenure, and repayment terms may vary by applicant profile.
  • Borrowers should complete KYC and income verification for a final decision.
